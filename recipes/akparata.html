Akparata Soup

How to make Akparata Soup

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Dryfish
  • Stockfish
  • Cameroun Pepper, ground (optional)
  • Scotch Bonnet, ground (fresh pepper)
  • 6 cups stock and water
  • 1 teaspoon uziza seed, ground (optional)
  • 6 Tablespoons ground crayfish
  • 1/2 cup Palm oil
  • 2 Tablespoons ground Akparata
  • 8-10 leaves Uziza leaves
  • 3 seasoning cubes
  • salt
  • 1 small bunch Ora leaves

Steps

  1. Wash meat and stockfish, place in a medium-sized pot. Season meat and stockfish with salt and seasoning cubes. Add water and cook over medium-high heat until tender.
  2. Place dry fish in boiling hot water for 1 minute, drain and rinse well in cold water, remove bones.
  3. Add dry fish, Cameroun pepper, fresh pepper and ground uziza seed. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, place akparata in a small bowl, pour in palm oil and mix very well to have a smooth paste.
  5. Pour akparata mixture into the boiling meat and stir. Cover pot, reduce heat to medium and cook for 3 minutes.
  6. Add crayfish and uziza leaves to boiling meat. Stir and taste, add salt.
  7. Cook for 5 minutes.
  8. Add Ora leaves, cook for 2 minutes and take off the heat.
  9. Serve with a swallow of choice.
